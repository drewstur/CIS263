Hail Purdue and Purdue Hymn

 

Hail Purdue (Chorus only)

Hail Purdue First Stanza: 
To your call once more we rally;
Alma Mater, hear our praise!
Where the Wabash spreads its valley
Filled with joy our voices raise;
From the skies in swelling echoes,
Come the cheers that tell the tale
Of your victories and your heroes,
Hail Purdue! We sing, All hail!

Hail Purdue Chorus:
Hail, Hail to Old Purdue
All Hail to our Old Gold and Black;
Hail, Hail to Old Purdue,
Our friendship may she never lack;
Ever grateful, ever true,
Thus we raise our song anew
Of the days we've spent with you;
All Hail, our own Purdue.

Purdue Hymn

Close by the Wabash in famed Hoosier land
Stands Old Purdue, serene and grand!
Cherished by memory
By all her sons and daughters true,
Fair Alma Mater, All Hail, Purdue,
Fairest in all the land,
Our own Purdue,
Fairest in all the land,
Our own Purdue.
